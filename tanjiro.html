Demon Slayer Vol. 4 | Tanjiro



Evidence



(Gotōge 114-115)


Explanation


This happened when the demon, the Tanjiro was ordered to slaughter the Demon Slayer Corps members. Rui was acting as a son and threatening the Mom with the name of Dad. As we can see through the sweats and "HFF"s, the Mom is afraid of Dad's interference. This is not only a portrayal of the cruelty and malevolence of the demon Rui, but also a reflection on traditional Japanese societal norms as this scene took place in the Early 20th century. Tanijro is soooooooo yasashii


Connection


One of the meanings of this part is to reveal the issues in the past Japanese society and help remember the past. By then, people believed in ageism and how males possessed significantly higher positions than females. Although society in Japan and where we live is modernized today, we still have to learn about the inequalities in the past to avoid unfair treatment from happening again.
